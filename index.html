What brings me to software engineering

I've always enjoyed playing with technology. I remember in elementary school getting to use a simple program to make a colored line go across the screen and playing the old school oregon trail video game. I've always been interested in what tech jobs were like and at home I love to tinker with apps, smart devices and using programs to help me learn more faster. I am hoping that learnig code will allow me to better understand the things I deal with and give me a marketable transferable skill I can use to find a technology job that will allow me to learn more and provide more time with and opportunities for my family. I found Coding Temple through Career Karma and found the bootcamp idea for a career change very interesting. With a full time job and family it was very important I find a program with a minimum amount of hours I can follow weekly and strong support to bridge the gap between self taught and an in person camp that I don't have time for.
